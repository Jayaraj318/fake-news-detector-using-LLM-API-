About Fake News Detection

Empowering you to spot misinformation with AI. Our platform leverages advanced language models to analyze news articles and help you make informed decisions.

How It Works

  • Paste or enter a news article on the Analyze page.
  • Our backend securely sends the text to Gemini LLM via FastAPI.
  • Receive a verdict (Real or Fake) with a clear, human-readable explanation.

Our Mission

In a world where misinformation spreads rapidly, our mission is to provide everyone with a simple, effective tool to verify news before sharing or believing it.

Who We Are

We are a team of AI researchers, developers, and journalists passionate about truth and digital literacy. Our goal is to make fact-checking accessible to all.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. We do not store your articles. All analysis is performed securely and anonymously.

How accurate is the AI?

Our system uses state-of-the-art language models and is continuously improved. However, always use your own judgment alongside our results.

Can I use this for any language?

Currently, we support English. More languages are coming soon!
